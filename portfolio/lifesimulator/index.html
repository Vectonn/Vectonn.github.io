This is a small 'life simulator'/digital pet game similar to Tamagotchis and other virtual pet idle games. It was a short project put together in a few days as part of a weekend game jam with a few friends.

This was primarily a way to introduce myself and become familiar with Godot for future projects.
Developed in Godot with GDScript for PC, it features:

Idle gameplay where you can check back periodically to see how your pet's doing.
Happiness and hunger systems for your pet.
Cleaning your pet's room to ensure they stay happy. (Currently represented by a placeholder sprite.)
An economy based on cleaning your pet's room.
A variety of food to feed your pet, each with different costs and hunger replenishment.
